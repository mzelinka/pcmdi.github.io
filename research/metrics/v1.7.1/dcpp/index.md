# Decadal Climate Prediction Project ([DCPP][dcpp])
<br/>


<br/>
### (PROTOTYPE) Summary statistics in Interactive Portrait Plot

This page provides prediction skill score analysis for simulations from Decadal Climate Prediction Project ([DCPP][dcpp]) as documented in Choi et al. (2026, in prep).

- Mean Bias (Figures 1 and 2 of Choi et al. 2026):
  - [Near-surface temperature][mean_bias_tas]
  - [Precipitation][mean_bias_pr]
  - [Arctic sea ice][mean_bias_sic_nh]
  - [Antarctic sea ice][mean_bias_sic_sh]

- Trend Bias (Figures 5 and 6 of Choi et al. 2026):
  - [Near-surface temperature][trend_bias_tas]
  - [Precipitation][trend_bias_pr]
  - [Arctic sea ice][trend_bias_sic_nh]
  - [Antarctic sea ice][trend_bias_sic_sh]

- Skill Score of Near-surface temperature (TAS) (Figure 9 of Choi et al. 2026)
  - [Anomaly correlation coefficient (ACC)][skill_sc_tas_acc]
  - [Mean squared skill score (MSSS)][skill_sc_tas_msss]
  - [Ratio of predictable components (RPC)][skill_sc_tas_rpc]


NOTE: Supported browsers for the Interactive Portrait Plots are Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Microsoft Edge. Microsoft Internet Explorer is no longer supported.
